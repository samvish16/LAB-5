Addendum: Stylistic Choices

Several stylistic choices were made to enhance the readability and visual appeal of the webpage:

  • Font Choices: 'Sacramento' was chosen for its elegant and decorative script style. The font size is set to 20px for headings and 16px for paragraphs to ensure readability.
  • Color Choices: The main text color is black (#000), while subheadings are light gray (#999) to provide a balance between aesthetics and readability. The gradient background enhances contrast with the text.
  • Border and Padding: A border of 2px solid black and padding of 10px is applied to the entire document to create a clear separation between the content and the edges of the browser window.
  • Heading and Text Sizes: The <h1> tag is used for the title for its prominence, while <h2> and <p> tags are used for subheadings and paragraphs, respectively, to maintain a hierarchical structure. Font sizes have been adjusted for readability and visual appeal.
